How to Use Cookies in Google Chrome or any another Browser

Step:1 Firstly, you need to download chrome extension By Clicking Here

Step:2 First Copy the cookies of your desired website by visitng getCookies

Step:3 Now Visit your desired website of which you have copied cookies and click on extension logo in address bar and click on cookie editor.

Step:4 Now Click on delete All Button

Step:5 Now CLick on Import Button next to delete all button

Step:6 Now Paste (CTRL+V) on in the box and click import button

Now Refresh the website by pressing F5 Key or by right click
